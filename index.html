About The Project

The main goal of our application is to enable communication between seniors and volunteers in a simple and intuitive way for both sides.

Senior sends SMS with his shopping list, which appears on announcement borad. Volunteers can pick requests from their neighbourhood and then deliver it to seniors' door.

How it works

1. Senior sends SMS to a given number with his shopping list along with the name of a district that he lives in.

  • Hi, I need 2l of water, 3 rolls and 1 bottle of cooking oil. I live in Gda≈Ñsk Zaspa.
  • 'Your request has been received. We are waiting for a volunteer to come forward.

2. A volunteer selects a shopping list with a suitable location from the announcement board.

3. A volunteer delivers shopping to a given address.

4. Senior is asked to give feedback. He can rate the service or report a complaint.

  • Your products should already be delivered, do you have any comments ?
