About The Project

The main goal of our application is and enable communication between seniors and volunteers in simple and intuitive way for both sides.

Communication is establiszed via annnouncment board where senior is able to request for shopping via SMS and volunteer, after contacting with senior, will bring them just to senior\'s door.

How it works

1. Senior send SMS with a list of needed grocery on provided telephone number.

  • Hi, I need 2l of water, 3 rolls, and 1 l of oil, i live in Gansk Zaspa
  • 'Your request has been collected. We are waiting for someone to take care of it

2. The volunteer finds the advertisement on the map and accepts the request

3. Volunteer provides the needed products

4. Delivery message has been sent to the senior

  • Your products should already be delivered, do you have any comments ?
  • Everything was perfect :)
